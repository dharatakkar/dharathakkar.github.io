Corporate office interiors

A full corporate campus fit-out for Care Group: executive offices, a manager's cabin, individual working cabins, and a second-floor conference and recreation hub. I carried the project from measured drawings of the existing structure, through space planning, 3D visualization, and material palette presentations, to daily site supervision and weekly client reviews until handover.

Design to Handover

01Second Floor: Conference & Recreation Hub

An open industrial shell reimagined as the social core of the office. The client asked for a conference area as the centre of attraction with seating for ten, a lounge, a play zone (carrom, table tennis, chess), a fully equipped gym, a pantry, and two shower rooms, with all-glass partitions and no civil changes. The conference zone is defined by a blue carpet inlay referencing the company logo, framed by glass partitions spanning the existing MS columns, with wooden-texture tile flooring (4 x 2 ft) throughout and the original windows and skylights preserved for daylight.

02Individual Working Cabins

First-floor working cabins designed around focus and storage: each station pairs an L-shaped work surface with full-height cabinets, a TV unit, and an attached washroom. Furniture is executed in plywood with 1 mm laminate in a white and wood finish, keeping the cabins bright, durable, and easy to maintain. Deliverables ran from the measured cabin plan through 3D views to on-site execution.

Cabin PlanningPlywood + Laminate3D VisualizationOn-site Execution

03Executive Offices: Final Views

Three completed executive offices, each with custom-built desks, fluted wood panelling, cove lighting, and backlit world-map feature walls. Lounge corners with statement seating (including a signature yellow leather sofa and framed artwork) soften the work zones, while consistent white-and-walnut joinery ties the three offices into one visual language.

Custom FurnitureCove LightingFeature WallsFF&E

04Manager's Cabin, Care Group

A compact cabin redesigned with minimal civil change around a cool colour palette (pistachio, toffee brown, cream white, wooden brown, slate grey). The working desk doubles as a three-person conference table in a toffee-brown Korean acrylic solid surface, with MDF wall panelling, wooden-texture tile flooring, plywood storage with 1 mm laminate, a single 4-inch POP ceiling layer, a wall-hung temple niche, and a toughened-glass entry. Scope covered measured drawings of the existing site, palette and material presentations, daily site visits, and weekly client reviews.

Korean Solid SurfaceMDF PanellingColour PaletteClient PresentationsSite Visits

05Chairman's Office & Reception

The senior-most space in the campus: an 8 x 3 ft working desk for the chairman, a conference table, a TV unit, and a custom award display panel fabricated in MS sheet and rod with a black powder-coat finish. A level change in the floor separates the working zone from the meeting zone, and the adjoining waiting area carries the brand's world-map wallpaper with lounge seating.
