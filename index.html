About

Design that endures

I'm an Interior Designer with over three years of experience delivering thoughtful, brand-aligned spaces across workplace, hospitality, and residential sectors. My work spans continents — from commercial projects in Gujarat, India, to high-end residential design in the New York tri-state area.

Currently at Crate & Barrel in New Jersey, I develop end-to-end design solutions — 2D & 3D floor plans, curated furniture presentations, and personalized client consultations across in-store, in-home, and virtual settings.

My master's thesis, Unseen Green, explored sustainable urban tourism through a prototype mobile application — reflecting my commitment to design that serves both people and planet.
